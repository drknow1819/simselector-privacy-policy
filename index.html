2. How We Use Information

  • App functionality: Power the four tabs—Dialer, Call History, Contacts, and Settings (SIM-prefix mapping).
  • Advertising: Serve relevant ads via Google AdMob using Advertising ID & approximate location.
  • Performance & reliability: Detect battery optimizations, background restrictions, and App Standby Bucket to keep call routing reliable.

3. Data Sharing

Google/AdMob receives only your Advertising ID and approximate location for ad delivery. No other data (call logs, contacts, usage state, installed apps) is shared.
