Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2025

SIM Selector is provided by SmartServe Labs and is ad-supported via Google AdMob.

1. Information We Collect

This app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal, call-log, contact, or location data. All call logs and contacts are read in-memory for display only; nothing is saved or shared by the app.

The embedded Google AdMob SDK may collect:

  • Advertising ID: to serve and target ads
  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID & other IDs): for ad delivery and measurement
  • Approximate location (IP-based): to personalize ads

2. How We Use Information

  • App features: Dialer calls, in-memory display of call logs & contacts, SIM-mapping settings.
  • Ads: Google AdMob uses collected IDs & location to deliver and measure ads.

3. Data Sharing

Only the Advertising ID, device identifiers, and approximate location are shared with Google/AdMob for advertising purposes. No other data is transmitted off-device.

4. Permissions Requested at Setup

Upon first launch, during the initial setup stage, the app will ask you to grant these permissions. Each request is accompanied by an on-screen explanation:

  • CALL_PHONE – to place calls via the correct SIM
  • READ_CALL_LOG / WRITE_CALL_LOG – to display and update recent calls in the Call History tab
  • READ_CONTACTS – to display your contact list in the Contacts tab
  • PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS – to check battery & background restrictions so the call-routing service remains active
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE – to keep the call-routing service running uninterrupted

No permission is requested silently; you must explicitly approve each one to proceed.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
